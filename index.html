Smart Windows



Smart Windows, also known as Switchable Smart Glasses, use advanced technologies to block sunlight and radiation coming from outside. The windowâ€™s light transmission properties can be affected by heat, electricity or sunlight. This means that the glass can change from transparent to translucent, blocking more than 70% of the light and radiation coming from the sun. Smart Glasses can be used to control light, heat, and privacy in both workspaces and personal spaces. Switchable Smart Windows provide cleaner environments and offer higher levels of control over light than objects such as curtains, shades, and doors.


There are six types of Smart Windows:
  • Electrochromic
  • Photochromic
  • Thermochromic
  • Suspended Particle
  • Micro Blind
  • Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystals
